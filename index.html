JEE Main 2022 College Predictor

Students who appear in the JEE Main exam with an aim to get admission into NITs, IIITs and GFTIs are often anxious regarding their admission. The JEE Main college predictor 2022 tool can act as a perfect tool to help them. EduExplore presents a JEE Main college predictor tool which will help you know the details about the best colleges and courses you can get on the basis of your JEE Main 2022 results.


With the help of the JEE Main 2022 college predictor tool, candidates can know the list of colleges respective to their scores and rank in the entrance exam. Using the JEE Main college predictor tool is easy as it requires students to just  certain details like the overall percentile, gender, home state and more.  JEE Main college predictor tool uses an advanced algorithm to predict the college based on candidates' score. 


Steps to use the JEE Main 2022 college predictor tool? 

In order to use the JEE Main college predictor 2022 tool, students have to submit the following details.

  • JEE Main Paper-1 Category Rank (Overall Rank if GEN)
  • Select your Home State 
  • Category
  • Gender
  • Specially Abled? No/Yes


Benefits of JEE Main college predictor tool

With the help of JEE Main college predictor tool you can get detailed information about all the colleges you might get admission in as per the scores. The JEE Main college predictor tool provide students with personalized reports with the list of  top predicted institutes directly to their inbox. Moreover, through the JEE Main 2022 college predictor tool, you get to know about not just NITs, IIITs and GFTIs, but also about the other good institutes that accept JEE scores and where you stand a chance too.
